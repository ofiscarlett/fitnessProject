BODY CALCULATOR

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a measurement is a good way to gauge whether your weight is in healthy proportion to your height. The Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR) estimates your basal metabolic rate—the amount of energy expended while at rest in a neutrally temperate environment, and in a post-absorptive state (meaning that the digestive system is inactive, which requires about 12 hours of fasting). The Required Calorie amount estimate the number of calories a person needs to consume each day to gain or lose or maintain the weight based on the activity level. But this is just an estimate.
